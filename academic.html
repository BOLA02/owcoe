Academic Programmes

Explore our diverse range of undergraduate and graduate programmes designed to prepare future educators and leaders in the field of education.

Undergraduate Programmes

Bachelor of Science in Elementary Education

This programme prepares students to teach in elementary school settings, focusing on child development, curriculum design, and effective teaching strategies.

Bachelor of Arts in Secondary Education

Specializing in various subjects, this programme equips students to teach in middle and high school environments, emphasizing subject-specific pedagogy.

Bachelor of Education in Special Education

This programme focuses on teaching students with diverse learning needs, covering inclusive education practices and individualized instruction.

Graduate Programmes

Master of Education in Educational Leadership

Designed for aspiring school leaders, this programme covers school administration, policy, and organizational management.

Master of Arts in Teaching

This programme offers advanced training for experienced teachers, focusing on curriculum development, assessment, and instructional leadership.

Doctor of Education in Higher Education

This doctoral programme prepares scholars and leaders in higher education, focusing on research, policy, and institutional effectiveness.

Double Major Options

Students can enhance their expertise by combining their education major with another field of study. Popular double major options include English, History, Mathematics, and Science. Contact an academic advisor for more information.

Entry Requirements

Admission to OWCOE programmes requires a high school diploma or equivalent, standardized test scores, letters of recommendation, and a personal essay. Specific requirements may vary by programme. Please refer to the Admissions section for detailed information.
