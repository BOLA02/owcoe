WELCOME TO OSWALD WALLER COLLEGE OF EDUCATION

Welcome to Oswald Waller College of Education (OWCOE), Lifidi, Plateau State. We are a professional Catholic teacher’s training college of the Catholic Diocese of Shendam. You have made the right decision in choosing our prestigious institution. Here at Oswald Waller, we use a holistic approach to form students intellectually, socially, morally, and spiritually. We imbue and empower our students to develop professional teaching skills and critical thinking. Our Programs and faculty facilitate a friendly and conducive learning environment. We prepare educators who strive to deliver quality education and enrich lives across a variety of academic and community settings. A good and effective teacher is an inestimable and indispensable value to any society. By choosing our institution, we are glad you are setting off on the right course to becoming one.
